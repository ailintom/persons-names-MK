The database

Persons and Names of the Middle Kingdom is an online database of Egyptian Middle Kingdom personal names and their bearers. It includes data on personal names, people bearing names, written sources where people are attested, dossiers of persons attestated in different sources.

The project

The online database was developed within the framework of the project “Umformung und Variabilität im Korpus altägyptischer Personennamen 2055–1550 v. Chr.” funded by the Fritz Thyssen Foundation.
